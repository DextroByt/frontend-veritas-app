Verification Process Explained

How Sentinel AI Verification Works (The Cognitive Architecture)

Sentinel AI utilizes a stateful, multi-agent process to verify claims, designed for speed and relibility. This is known as the "Cognitive Architecture" and runs continuously in the background.

1. Parallel Evidence Gathering

Upon receiving a claim, the system simultaneously dispatches it to three specialized agents (reducing "Time to Verdict"):

Official Source Agent

  • Checks high-trust domains (e.g., NDRF, local police).
  • Uses strict keyword intersection to confirm official status.

Media Cross-Referencer

  • Queries trusted global and local media outlets (NewsAPI).
  • Filters for recency (last 7 days) to ensure current crisis relevance.

Debunker Agent

  • Targets known fact-checking sites (e.g., AltNews).
  • Uses similarity scoring to flag potential refutations.

2. LLM-Driven Synthesis

Finally, the Synthesizer (powered by Gemini) acts as the "Chief Verification Officer." It aggregates all evidence and applies strict logic to determine the final, color-coded verdict (VERIFIED, DEBUNKED, or UNCONFIRMED). The output is structured and ready for immediate database insertion and display on this timeline.
